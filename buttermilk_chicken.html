Buttermilk Chicken

Description

Fried chicken, need I say more?

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Buttermilk, to coat
  • Flour 150 g
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Leave the chicken in the buttermilk in the fridge overnight.
  2. Mix the flour, paprika, cayenne pepper, garlic powder, salt and pepper in a plate.
  3. Shake the excess buttermilk off the chicken and coat the chicken in the seasoned flour.
  4. Heat 2cm of oil in a pan.
  5. Fry the chicken for 25-30 minutes or until cooked through.
